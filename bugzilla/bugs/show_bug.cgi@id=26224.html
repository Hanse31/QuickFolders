Description:   Opened: 2016-05-09 04:37 
I was able to left click and email, drag it to the QF tab I wanted and a list
of the sub folders would appear and I could move the email to the sub-folder I
wanted.  Now, when I drag any email to any QF tab, I do not get the drop-down
list of sub-folders and can not move the email.

------- Comment #2 From Axel Grude 2016-05-09 04:42:33 ------- 
Created an attachment (id=8183) [details]
4.4.3 prerelease 43

SFixes the issue for he following cases:

1) dragging an email to a tab to move it
2) dragging an email to a tab and subfolder from a popup
3) dragging an email to a tab and "new Subfolder..." via popup menu


Apparently the "dragSession" in the drop event doesn't contain the necessary
information anymore so I am getting it from event.dropData instead. Tested in
Tb Release (45) and Daily (48.0a2 (2016-05-08))

Needs to be regression tested against SeaMonkey and Postbox!
