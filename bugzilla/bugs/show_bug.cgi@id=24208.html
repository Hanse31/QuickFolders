Description:   Opened: 2011-07-15 05:23 
I use a lot the sub-folders feature provided by the QuickFolders add-on.

However, moving emails to such sub-folders via drag-n-drop sometimes fails with
two different outcomes.

In the first case, the drop target seems to get completely lost and the email
will simply stay where it was.

In the second and worse case, another sub-folder (mostly one on the way to the
considered sub-folder) is taken as the drop target.

Especially, the second case is anoying as only the activities view will show
you where your email was accidentially dropped.
