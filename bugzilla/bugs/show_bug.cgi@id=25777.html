I think the keyboard shortcuts in Thunderbird can break under certain
circumstances, but I believe this is not something specific to QuickFolders. I
have other extensions (Zombie Keys) where this can be experienced as well.If
you want to monitor errors related to this, you can certainly leave console
running, but you might have to clear the output once i a while as I think it
slows down Tb when it fills up too much or can truncate the log, or stop
logging so that you loose the error messages you are interested in. 

Also it is likely that once the shortcuts don't work they won't actually
trigger any log messages - the observers can just simply be removed or disabled
or broken and then do nothing.
