Description:   Opened: 2016-07-20 10:28

produce an empty email. In the previous version the link was inserted at the
bottom of the email after the template / signature.

In the new behavior smartTemplate tries to bypasses the template and is
intended to replace the complete body to avoid losing information from the web
site.
