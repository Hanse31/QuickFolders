Yes, I meant a toggle for all/unread. I've looked all through the manuals and
searched everywhere and found nothing on this. And it's hard to do anything at
Postbox, except see the FAQs and Docs.

The only way I can toggle all/unread is to open the focus window and click on
all or filter to unread (that I have found). The window takes up a huge amount
of space. There are no keyboard shortcuts, and it doesn't always refresh.
Sometimes you have to click on another folder to get it to refresh your view.

In digging through FAQs at Postbox, I'm not the only one that has requested an
icon that toggles between all/unread. 

But since you have a toolbar there that you can add things to (and it works
great), I thought it would be a nice place to add the icon that toggles a
folder view between all and unread (and refreshes the view after doing so).

If you know something I missed, please let me know. But based on a few FAQs,
I'm not the only one that missed it. 

Thanks.
