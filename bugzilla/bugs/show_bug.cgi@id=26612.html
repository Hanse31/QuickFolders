That's right. In case the auto-update doesn't work (if you have already a
higher number installed or there are problems with contacting the update
service) the manual route is:

1) open the Add-ons Manager in Thunderbird
2) drag the attachment link above  (4.12.1 Official Release) ito the Add-ons
Manager.

(alternatively, right-click the attachment link, save as file, and then drag
the file into the Add-ons Manager)

the drag and drop should also work from 
https://addons.thunderbird.net/thunderbird/addon/quickfolders-tabbed-folders/

by dragging the green "download now" button into the Add-on Manager.

Apart from updating, this is the quickest way once you get your head around it.
