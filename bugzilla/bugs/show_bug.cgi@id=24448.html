Running QF 2.8.3pre16 on TBird 7.0, WinXP SrvIII.

After TBird update to version 7.0, Add-ons Mangler reporting "QuickFolders is
incompatible with Thunderbird 7.0", "QuickFolders 2.8.3pre16 (disabled)".
------- Comment #1 From Tony Mechelynck 2011-09-29 02:31:42 ------- 
Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64; rv:10.0a1) Gecko/20110928 Firefox/10.0a1
SeaMonkey/2.7a1 ID:20110928003003

Even after the recent version chenge, QuickFolders 2.8.3pre16 works for me with
a version override, it should work even on Thuhnderbird 10.0a1 except for
"paperwork incompatibility".

As a workaround, you can install the Add-on Compatibility Reporter extension
(and restart Thunderbird twice in the process) but beware that it will then
become your responsibility to manually disable any actually misbehaving
extension: maxVersion mismatches will be mentioned in the addons manager but
not acted upon.

The ACR suppresses auto-disabling of paperwork-incompatible extensions, and it
also allows the user to report that an add-on still works despite paperwork
incompatibility, or that an add-on has problems even though it boasts
compatibility.

Once addons.mozilla.org get their bugs ironed out, add-ons hosted there will
see their maxVersion bumped automatically every six weeks, unless there is a
reason not to do it (such as use of a recently removed entry point, or use of
binary libraries over XPCOM). IIUC, this already applies to Firefox maxVersion
settings, and the intention is to do it for Thunderbird and SeaMonkey too. I
think (but I may be wrong) that it has been done for Thunderbird and will soon
be done for SeaMonkey.

For addons not hosted at AMO, bumping the maxVersion remains the addon
developer's job.
