Description:   Opened: 2011-06-13 03:43 
I have email accounts with serveral providers and have created a Quick-Folders
Tab for each of the inboxes. It would be great if after having read all new
mails in one tab I could open the next tab by clicking a button on the
active-file tool and/or open the next unread mail in any Quick-Folder Tab by a
special button. Would such features be of interest to any other users? Ernst
