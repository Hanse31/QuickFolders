Description:   Opened: 2018-08-15 13:56 
Thunderbird 60 (Release and Betas)
QuickFolders 4.10 and several betas
Multiple IMAP folders synced from Outlook 365 in a single account

Simple but random and a little intermittent. Switching to a folder, doesn't.

 1. Left or right click on a folder tab.
 2. Mouse down to the drop-down list
 3a. List closes as soon as the mouse pointer leaves the tab and enters the
menu
 3b. Or, list doesn't close but attempts to mouse across to a sub-folder result
in the menu closing
 3c. Sometimes, mouse off the tab and onto the list, and any sub-folders, and
all appears to work fine. Until you select a folder. Nothing happens other than
the list closes.
 3d. Occasionally you can go through the same process and it works! You can
change to the selected folder, just as advertised!

There seems to be neither rhyme nor reason to this. Sometimes it works fine
although more often than not it doesn't. Trying twice often doesn't help,
thrice might do. Basically, try often enough and eventually it'll work. If you
have the patience.

Folder lists. Some are pretty short, some are short with a few sub-folders. One
is  pretty massive - and that is the one folder that *always* seems to work.
There are sufficient folders here to fill the screen from top to below the
bottom... I suspect this folder always works because with a left or right click
on the tab the mouse pointer is placed directly into the folder list.

Did this ever work as advertised? Yes, on Thunderbird pre-60 (59?) and QF
4.9.1. Several TB 60 Beta versions have been tried, all with no improvement. QF
4.10 and betas were also no help.

In conclusion. Massively useful plug-in; with 30G+ of email, a daily deluge of
new mail and countless folders it is a life saver for quick filing and
navigation around those folders. And pre- TB 60 it worked fine, I just wish it
still did :(

Note. My two primary requirements/uses are just:
 1. Drag mail from InBox to a destination folder through QF. That always works
fine.
 2. Right click on QF tab and select "Open in New Tab" followed by Left or
Right click on the same QF tab to navigate to the required sub-folder (as
required).
