Description:   Opened: 2017-12-13 10:59 
12-13-17

I very much appreciate quickFilters.  I have been using the basic/free version
for a while. Thank you very much for sharing it. 

Recently, however, it has begun automatically (i.e., unwantedly) processing
messages whenever I highlight them with the mouse--even though all I wanted to
do was read the selected message.  By "processing," I mean that quickFilters
appears to be moving the selected message, and sometimes also the messages that
automatically gain the highlight/focus of the program on account of the removal
of the selected message.  No error message or any other notification is made by
Thunderbird when this happens.  The affected message(s) simply disappears.

I am not entirely sure to where those messages are being moved.  Some of them
made their way into the "Sent" folder.  Presumably/possibly, that is because
they were unsent/draft messages that I created and saved for future use. The
processing seems to take place no matter what folder I am using.  It has
happened to messages in Thunderbird's "Drafts" folder, as well as other folders
that I created for message storage purposes. (It does not appear that any new
filters have been created by this activity.)

This unwanted activity ceased when I deactivated quickFolders. And that is
where things are for me at this time.

Please feel free to contact me if I can be of further assistance to you in
resolving this matter.

PS:  I use Windows 10 Pro.
