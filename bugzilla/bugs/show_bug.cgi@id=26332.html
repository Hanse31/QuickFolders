------- Comment #3 From Axel Grude 2017-01-31 03:14:51 -------

This does not apply to QF tabs - they open folders, which are essentially
listing email headers. These headers are stored locally unless you specify not
to download anything on IMAP. 

The biggest speed improvement you can achieve is in Account Settings /
Synchronization & Storage by checking the top checkbox

"Keep messages for this account on this computer"

and the next radio button

"Synchronize all messages locally regardless of age"

Messages are downloaded automatically in the background - way before you click
on the tab. When you click on a folder tab it only needs to be fetched from the
hard drive, and that cannot be significantly accelerated because it doesn't
have the network bottleneck (downloading online content) which instantclick
deals with.

As regards preloading web content on *previewed email messages*  by hovering in
the thread pane (top right of the 3pane window) this is well out of scope for
QuickFolders.
