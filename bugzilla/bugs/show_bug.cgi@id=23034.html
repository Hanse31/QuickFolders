Description:   Opened: 2010-07-20 05:20 
v1.93 has some new features re hovering over a tab, such as the orange color
and a small change in the tab's height.  One annoying consequence is that the
height of the whole QF toolbar also changes a little as you pass the mouse on
and off of it, making the whole window below it "jitter."  It's not a major
problem, but it always catches my attention when it happens, and the question
"What was that?" comes into my head.  A possible fix would be to have the
toolbar display the larger height at all times, but still let the tab change
height if that's what you want it to do.

   I suppose you already know that the tab's color does NOT change to the
"hover" color when if the tab has previously been manually colored by the user,
so I won't enter that as a separate bug.  That may have been your design re
color priorities.

------- Comment #8 From Darren Turpin 2010-07-27 00:16:56 ------- 
I was having the same problem with v1.93 on my installation, but I think I
found a fix (for my Win XP machine, at least):

Via: c:\Documents and Settings\Owner\Application
Data\Thunderbird\Profiles\[blah].default\extensions\quickfolder@curious.be\chrome\content

Open layout.css to editor

On line 141, change 

padding: 0px 5px 2px 2px;

to

padding: 0px 5px 0px 2px;

Save layout.css and re-start Thunderbird.

No more jitter (not on my machine, anyhow).
